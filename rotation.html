Windwalker Monk 10.0.5 Guide

General Information

Windwalker Monks use a priority system instead of a "traditional" rotation. This means that you should use the highest priority skill that is at your disposal and avoid substituting a lower priority ability for a higher priority one.

Single Target Priority

This is going to be your main priority list for some raid encounters, pure single target fights.

  1. Faeline Stomp
  2. Strike of the Windlord
  3. Whirling Dragon Punch
  4. Rising Sun Kick if Xuen's Battlegear crit buff is active
  5. Fists of Fury
  6. Rising Sun Kick
  7. Spinning Crane Kick with Dance of Chi-Ji damage buff
  8. Blackout Kick do not overcap on Teachings of the Monastery
  9. Rushing Jade Wind
  10. Chi Wave
  11. Chi Burst

Cleave Priority List (2-4 Targets)

This is going to be your main priority list for some raid fights with adds (most of them), Mythic+ dungeons with relatively small pulls (Up until +10)

  1. Faeline Stomp
  2. Strike of the Windlord
  3. Spinning Crane Kick with Dance of Chi-Ji damage buff
  4. Fists of Fury
  5. Whirling Dragon Punch
  6. Rising Sun Kick
  7. Spinning Crane Kick during Bonedust Brew
  8. Blackout Kick do not overcap on Teachings of the Monastery
  9. Rushing Jade Wind
  10. Rising Sun Kick
  11. Spinning Crane Kick
  12. Chi Burst

AOE (5+ Targets)

This is going to be your main priority list for Mythic+ dungeons with huge packs. (Higher keys)

  1. Faeline Stomp
  2. Strike of the Windlord
  3. Spinning Crane Kick with Dance of Chi-Ji damage buff
  4. Fists of Fury
  5. Whirling Dragon Punch and Rising Sun Kick
  6. Rushing Jade Wind
  7. Blackout Kick do not overcap on Teachings of the Monastery
  8. Rising Sun Kick
  9. Chi Burst
  10. Chi Wave
