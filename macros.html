MACROS

Detox Mouseover

If the target of your mouseover action is a friendly player and they are not already dead, using this macro will cast Detox on them.

#showtooltip Detox
/cast [@mouseover,help,nodead] Detox;

Copy Macro

Tiger's Lust Mouseover

If the target of your mouseover action is a friendly player and they are not already dead, using this macro will cast Tiger's Lust on them.

#showtooltip Tiger's Lust
/cast [@mouseover,help,nodead] Tiger's Lust;

Copy Macro

Bonedust Brew Quick Cast

This macro will throw Bonedust Brew at your cursor's location without having the click on the ground.

#showtooltip
/cast [known: 386276, @cursor] Bonedust Brew;

Copy Macro

Serenity, RSK Macro

This macro will cast Serenity and cast Rising Sun Kick at the same time, with just one button press.

#showtooltip SerenityRSK
/cast Serenity
/cast Rising Sun Kick
